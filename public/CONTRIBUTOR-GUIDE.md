# Neural Monolith — Contributor Quick Guide

One page. Read it before you title, describe, or tag anything. The goal of every choice below
is the same: help YouTube + a scrolling human instantly get what the video is and search-find it.

## What the channel is (never drift from this)
The **physical machinery underneath the AI era** — power plants, reactors, datacenters, undersea
cables, chips, supply chains. Tagline: *"The systems nobody shows you, explained properly."*
No news. No tutorials. No hype. Audience: US / CA / UK / W-Europe. Uploads: **Mon / Wed / Fri.**

## Titles (this is 80% of the click)
1. Lead with the surprising **physical fact**, not a vague tease. Front-load the keyword.
2. Use a proven structure:
   - "How [big thing] Powers / Is Rebuilding [system]" — *How Undersea Cables Power the Entire Internet*
   - "Why [Microsoft/Amazon/Google/Meta] Is [surprising action]" — a Big-Tech name = built-in search.
   - Named deep-dive: "[Three Mile Island / Oklo / Colossus] Explained".
   - Personal cost (most shareable): "…It's on Your Electric Bill."
3. One scale/shock word max: Massive, World's Largest, Hidden, Overtaxing. Never clickbait we can't pay off.
4. Keep it ≤ ~60 chars visible. Put the real search phrase in the first half.
5. Shorts title = one visceral fact ("One anchor can cut 95% of the world's data").

## Descriptions (first 150 chars are what search reads)
1. **First line = the exact question a viewer would type**, answered in one sentence.
   e.g. *"How does the internet cross the ocean? Nearly all data travels through undersea cables…"*
   Pull real phrases from `memory/longtail-keywords.md` — don't invent them.
2. Then: 2–3 sentence hook → "This is Under the Floor, Episode N…" → bullet list of what's revealed.
3. Always include: series links, `📚 CHAPTERS` (real timestamps), `🔗 SOURCES` (name the outlet).
4. Close with the subscribe line + "New episodes Monday, Wednesday & Friday."
5. Say the first-line keyword **out loud in the first 10 seconds** — YouTube indexes the transcript.

## Tags (minor, but free)
5–8 specific phrases from the long-tail bank + `Under the Floor` + `Neural Monolith`. Stay ≤ 500 chars.
Specific beats broad ("submarine cables" > "technology"). No keyword stuffing.

## Every upload — non-negotiable checklist
- [ ] Category = **Science & Technology**  ·  Language = **English (US)**
- [ ] Custom thumbnail: bold 2–3 word text + ONE clear object, high contrast (match existing style)
- [ ] Add to its **series playlist** (see below)
- [ ] **AI use → Yes** in the Checks step (we use AI imagery + a synthetic voice — required, avoids strikes)
- [ ] Not made for kids  ·  Comments On (sort: Top)  ·  Publish to subs feed = On

## Series playlists (pick one per video)
**Under the Floor** (flagship) · **One Anchor Away** (fragility / single points of failure) ·
**What It Actually Costs** (dollars + megawatts + water) · **Shorts** (all vertical clips).

## The bar
If a title/desc could belong to any generic tech channel, it's wrong. Ours always names a *specific
physical thing* and *why it matters to the viewer*. When unsure, check `memory/competitive-analysis.md`.
