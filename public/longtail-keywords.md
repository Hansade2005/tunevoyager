## Deployment rule
1. Pick ONE long-tail phrase per video → build the TITLE around it (front-loaded).
2. Put the exact question phrase in the FIRST LINE of the description.
3. Say it out loud in the first 10 seconds (YouTube indexes the transcript).
4. Add 3-5 of the phrases to that video's tags. See [[competitive-analysis]] for title patterns.
